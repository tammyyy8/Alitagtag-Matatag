Invention Dela Sta.Cruz

Batangas is often a go-to destination because of the many things you can do here. One of the churches to visit is the Invencion de la Sta. Cruz Parish in Alitagtag.Its façade has intricate designs, columns, and statues. Inside you’ll see beautiful religious icons and paintings. The atmosphere is quiet, ideal for contemplation and prayer.Wherever we see the Word of God purely preached and heard, there a church of God exists, even if it swarms with many faults. The perfect church service, would be one we were almost unaware of. Our attention would have been on God.
